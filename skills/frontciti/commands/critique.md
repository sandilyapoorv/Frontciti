# /frontciti critique

## Purpose

Provide a design-director critique of a frontend surface.

## Use When

Use when the user asks whether a design is good, why it feels off, what to improve, or whether it looks AI-generated.

## Inputs To Inspect

The target UI, source code, screenshots, product context, design system, register, copy, states, responsive behavior, and any automated findings.

## Workflow

1. Resolve the exact target.
2. Inspect the interface visually and structurally.
3. Judge register fit, hierarchy, IA, cognitive load, emotional fit, typography, color, layout, copy, states, accessibility, and AI tells.
4. Separate design critique from technical audit findings.
5. Prioritize the 3-5 most impactful issues.

## Output Expectations

Lead with an anti-slop verdict, overall design health score, what works, priority issues, why each matters, concrete fixes, and suggested next Frontciti commands.

## Anti-Patterns

- Saying "make it cleaner" without implementation guidance.
- Treating detector or lint output as a design critique.
- Listing every small issue with equal weight.

## Acceptance Checklist

- Critique is specific and prioritized.
- User impact is clear.
- Recommendations map to actionable fixes.
