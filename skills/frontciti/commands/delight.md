# /frontciti delight

## Purpose

Add tasteful personality, memorable details, or moments of joy without harming usability.

## Use When

Use when the interface works but feels bland, lifeless, or forgettable.

## Inputs To Inspect

Brand personality, user context, emotional moments, empty states, success moments, microinteractions, visuals, copy, and motion opportunities.

## Workflow

1. Identify one or two moments where delight serves the user.
2. Choose a detail rooted in product subject matter.
3. Prefer real content, visuals, motion, copy, or feedback over decorative effects.
4. Keep the rest of the interface disciplined.
5. Respect reduced motion and performance.

## Output Expectations

The result should feel more human and memorable while staying credible and usable.

## Anti-Patterns

- Confetti everywhere.
- Decorative motion with no purpose.
- Random jokes in serious workflows.
- Visual novelty that clashes with the brand.

## Acceptance Checklist

- Delight is localized and purposeful.
- Usability is unchanged or improved.
- Brand fit is clear.
