# /frontciti bolder

## Purpose

Make a design more memorable, premium, distinctive, expressive, or high-conviction.

## Use When

Use when the UI is clear enough but feels forgettable, generic, timid, or too close to default templates.

## Inputs To Inspect

Brand tone, audience expectations, current hierarchy, typography, color, imagery, copy, section shapes, motion, competitors, and anti-references.

## Workflow

1. Identify what must remain clear and credible.
2. Choose one or two strong visual or conceptual moves.
3. Sharpen hero concept, typography, contrast, imagery, copy, section rhythm, or motion.
4. Preserve usability and product trust.
5. Remove weaker decorative moves that compete with the main idea.

## Output Expectations

The result should be more memorable without becoming chaotic or less usable.

## Anti-Patterns

- Making everything loud.
- Adding many unrelated effects.
- Sacrificing readability for style.
- Using novelty that does not fit the product.

## Acceptance Checklist

- Design is more distinctive.
- Product clarity remains.
- Visual choices feel intentional.
- Credibility is preserved.
