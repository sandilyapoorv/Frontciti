# Responsive Design

Responsive design is not just stacking.

## Rules

- Identify what matters most on small screens.
- Collapse navigation deliberately.
- Preserve primary actions.
- Avoid tiny tap targets.
- Give tables mobile strategies.
- Prevent cards from becoming endless walls.
- Simplify charts on mobile when needed.
- Treat modals as sheets on mobile when appropriate.
- Test common breakpoints and content overflow.
